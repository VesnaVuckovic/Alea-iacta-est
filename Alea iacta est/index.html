Game rules

Rolling the dice for two players, who roll one die, which has values ​​from 1 to 6. Click on "Who plays first?" to decide who plays first. The player who is selected rolls the dice and then starts counting points until gets a 6 or a 1. If gets a 6, then the roll of the dice goes to the other player, and the sum will collected without a last six points, and the next roll begins with the collected points and a sum of zero for the new hand of the roll. If rolled a 1, that hand's total will reset to 0. During the game, by clicking on "collect", players can transfer the current score to the total score and save points.The first player who collect 101 points wins.
